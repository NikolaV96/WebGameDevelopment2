About

“Ball Blaster” will be an online browser game where players will be able to compete against each other in the form of a free to play 2D platformer. The objective of the game is for players to score as much as possible points by firing at incoming waves of enemies, while simultaneously dodging enemies.

FAQ

The objective of the game is for the players to destroy the incoming waves of enemies.The more damage they do the enemies, the higher the score will be.


The movement in the game will be handled by using A and D or left and right arrow keys on the keyboard for movement.


Firing at an enemy will be done by pressing on the space bar key or the up-arrow key.

Destroying a mystery object will drop one of four surprises: extra live, money, temporary freeze time or invulnerability.
